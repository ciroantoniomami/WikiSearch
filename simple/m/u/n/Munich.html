Munich

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Coat of arms Map
Frauenkirche and Town Hall

Munich (German: München) is the third biggest city of Germany (after Berlin and Hamburg), and the capital of Bavaria. It has a population of 1,320,000. The metropolitan area of Munich includes the city itself, and all the suburbs around it, and has about 2.6 million people in it. It is one of the most important center for economy in Germany.

[edit] People of Munich

About 23% of the people living in the city are foreigners. Most of these came from Turkey, Croatia, Serbia and Greece. About 60% of the people in Munich are Catholic, about 20% are Protestant, and about 7% belong to other religious groups. The other 13% gave no religion

[edit] Public Transportation

Public transport network

Munich has one of the most comprehensive systems in the world. There are subways, suburban trains, trams and buses. There might be built a Transrapid from the city center to the airport.
