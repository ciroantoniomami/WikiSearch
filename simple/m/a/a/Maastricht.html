Maastricht

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Old town of Maastricht looking over the river Maas.
Location of Maastricht in the Netherlands.

Maastricht is a city in the south of the Netherlands. About 120,000 people live there. Maastricht has an airport and a university.

The first settlement in that region was founded in the Stone age, the first permanent settlement was founded by the Celts about 500 B.C. Maastricht got City Rights in 1204. Since 1815, after the Napoleonic Wars, Maastricht belongs to the Netherlands. The European Contract of 1992 was signed in Maastricht.
