[edit] Season 1: 1999-2000

# Airdate Title Overview
1 May 1, 1999 Help Wanted SpongeBob is trained by two employees at the Krusty Krab to get his official job.
Reef Blower SpongeBob attempts to rid his lawn of a shell (3 minutes long).
Tea at the Treedome SpongeBob meets a squirrel and attempts to survive the stress of her treedom of air.
2 July 17, 1999 Bubblestand SpongeBob and Patrick run a bubble stand, which Squidward hopes to shut down.
Ripped Pants A consistent pant-ripping act performed by SpongeBob populates the citizens of Goo Lagoon.
3 July 31, 1999 Jellyfishing SpongeBob and Patrick take Squidward, who is recovering from a bike accident, on a jellyfishing trip.
Plankton! Mr. Krabs' arch rival makes the effort of controlling Spongebob in order to get his hands on the Krusty Krab's precious sandwich.
4 August 7, 1999 Naughty Nautical Neighbors Squidward plays a trick of insult on SpongeBob and Patrick so they can split up, but they begin a battle over Squidward.
Boating School SpongeBob wishes to pass his driving test with the help of a secret communication test.
5 August 14, 1999 Pizza Delivery SpongeBob and Squidward are sent by Mr. Krabs to deliver a pizza.
Home Sweet Pineapple Nematodes eat SpongeBob's pineapple house.
6 August 21, 1999 Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy SpongeBob and Patrick meet the retired actors who portrayed the heroes on their favorite T.V. show.
Pickles SpongeBob unmindfully abandoned pickles on a sea bass sandwich.
7 August 28, 1999 Hall Monitor SpongeBob plans to be a hall monitor, but he accidentally becomes a city criminal.
Jellyfish Jam SpongeBob adopts a jellyfish pet, who has an interest in music.
8 September 4, 1999 Sandy's Rocket SpongeBob and Patrick launch Sandy's rocket.
Squeaky Boots Mr. Krabs hands SpongeBob a pair of squeaky boots after he brought the wrong boots for his daughter's birthday.
9 September 11, 1999 Nature Pants SpongeBob goes to live wild among the jellyfish.
Opposite Day Squidward feigns an opposite day to SpongeBob and Patrick so a realtor will not notice their annoyance.
10 September 18, 1999 Culture Shock The Krusty Krab holds a talent show.
F.U.N. Plankton forgets his mission to steal the Krabby Patty Formula after having too much fun with SpongeBob.
11 September 25, 1999 MuscleBob BuffPants SpongeBob gets a fake pair of muscles so he can be populated throughout the city of Bikini Bottom.
Squidward the Unfriendly Ghost SpongeBob and Patrick serve Squidward's "ghost" after thinking that they accidentally killed him.
12 October 2, 1999 The Chaperone SpongeBob becomes Pearl's prom date.
Employee of the Month Squidward and SpongeBob compete for the Employee of the Month award.
13 October 28, 1999 Scaredy Pants SpongeBob dresses up as the Flying Dutchman to be scary after he himself got scared.
I Was a Teenage Gary Squidward agrees to take care of Gary while SpongeBob and Patrick are at a jellyfishing convention for three days, but ends up neglecting him.
14 December 31, 1999 SB-129 Squidward ends up in the chrome future.
Karate Choppers SpongeBob lets his considerations into fighting Sandy, which comes to scaring the residents of Bikini Bottom.
15 January 17, 2000 Sleepy Time SpongeBob visits the dreams of the other residents of Bikini Bottom.
Suds SpongeBob catches the suds.
16 February 14, 2000 Valentine's Day SpongeBob promises a great Valentine's Day gift for Patrick, but Sandy takes more time than he expected to deliver it, and Patrick cannot wait much longer.
The Paper Squidward gives SpongeBob a gum wrapper and tells him that he does not want it back, but when he sees all the fun that SpongeBob is having with it, he changes his mind.
17 March 15, 2000 Arrgh! Mr. Krabs leads SpongeBob and Patrick on a treasure hunt.
Rock Bottom SpongeBob gets stuck in the town of Rock Bottom.
18 March 22, 2000 Texas Sandy becomes homesick, and decides to return to Texas.
Walking Small SpongeBob tries to follow Plankton's steps of assertiveness but act the opposite.
19 April 1, 2000 Fools in April SpongeBob's April Fools Day pranks anger Squidward, so he decides to play a trick on SpongeBob, but goes too far.
Neptune's Spatula SpongeBob enters a cooking competition against King Neptune so he can be a master to cook for him.
20 April 8, 2000 Hooky SpongeBob and Patrick get into the dangerous interest of playing with fishing hooks.
Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy II Spongebob hustles a calling conch and Mermaid Man patrolls in a group addition to end the major responsibility.

Artist Unknown SpongeBob becomes a great artist which makes Squidward mad.

The Bully A bully threatens to beat SpongeBob up.
38 October 12 2001 Squid on Strike SpongeBob and Squidward go on strike.
Sandy, SpongeBob, and the Worm SpongeBob builds up crazy obstacles and tricks to stop Sandy from fighting a huge worm.
39 November 30 2001 Procrastination SpongeBob procrastinates after he is assigned an essay by Mrs. Puff.
I'm with Stupid SpongeBob feigns stupidity so Patrick's parents will think their son is smart.

# Airdate Title Overview
S1 2002 Nicktoons Battle A parody of Dragon Ball Z, which only featured Patrick "Danger" Star, among other Nicktoons, Tommy and Dil Pickles, Chuckie Finster, Debbie Thornberry, Helga Pataki, and Timmy Turner.
40 January 21 2002 Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy IV SpongeBob shrinks the entire town after "borrowing" Mermaid Man's belt.
Doing Time Mrs. Puff is sent to jail and is glad she does not have to deal with SpongeBob anymore, but he and Patrick try to convince her to leave.
41 January 21 2002 Welcome to the Chum Bucket Mr. Krabs bets SpongeBob in a card game against Plankton and loses.
Frankendoodle An evil drawing threatens SpongeBob and Patrick.
42 February 22 2002 Snowball Effect SpongeBob, Patrick, and Squidward start a snowball fight.
One Krabs Trash Mr. Krabs sells a soda drink hat to Spongebob and tries to get it back for a high price.
43 March 1 2002 Nasty Patty Mr. Krabs panics over a health inspection.
Idiot Box SpongeBob and Patrick play in a T.V. box and Squidward strangely hears realistic noises.
44 March 8 2002 As Seen on TV Fame goes to SpongeBob's head after making a brief appearance on T.V.
Can You Spare a Dime? Squidward quits working for Mr. Krabs after being blamed for stealing his dime. So, Spongebob lets him stay in his house, but Squidward takes advantage of him.
45 March 15 2002 No Weenies Allowed SpongeBob faces obstacles to enter a club which requires people with more strength than him.
Squilliam Returns Squidward turns the Krusty Krab into a 5 star restaurant to impress his rival Squilliam.
46 March 22 2002 The Algae's Always Greener Plankton uses a machine to switch lives with Mr. Krabs.
SpongeGuard on Duty SpongeBob becomes Goo Lagoon's lousy lifeguard.
47 March 29 2002 Krab Borg SpongeBob watches a scary robot movie on TV late at night, and the next day he starts believing that Mr. Krabs is a robot.
Rock-a-Bye Bivalve SpongeBob and Patrick find a baby scallop and decide to raise it, but soon Patrick makes SpongeBob do all the work.
48 May 10 2002 Wet Painters Mr. Krabs gives SpongeBob and Patrick the job of painting the walls in his house, with the strict instruction to not get paint on anything else, because it's permanent. When they get paint on Mr. Krabs' first dollar, they panic.
Krusty Krab Training Video A training narration of the Krusty Krab.
49 May 17 2002 Party Pooper Pants SpongeBob gets locked out of his house for a party night after being a terrible host, and everybody wrecks his house.
50 June 1 2002 Chocolate with Nuts SpongeBob and Patrick start to sell chocolate bars to live a fancy life.
Mermaid Man and Barnacle Boy V Barnicle Boy is being treated immaturely, so he becomes the opposite of a hero and joins Man Ray and the Dirty Bubble.
51 July 12 2002 Club SpongeBob SpongeBob and Patrick host a club in the kelp forest.
My Pretty Seahorse SpongeBob becomes the friend of a homeless seahorse.
52 September 6, 2002 Graveyard Shift Squidward tries to frighten SpongeBob by telling him about the "Hash Slinging Slasher," but soon Squidward becomes scared when the story seems to be coming true.
Krusty Love Mr. Krabs falls in love with Mrs. Puff, but soon begins buying her expensive gifts and blaming SpongeBob for spending his money.
53 September 20 2002 New Student Starfish Patrick attends Mrs. Puff's boating school, but gets them both in detention.
Clams Mr. Krabs tries to retrieve his millionth dollar after it was eaten by a clam, and Squidward and SpongeBob agree to help him. But things become a wreck after the two employees stay on the boat for days.
S2 December 4, 2002 Patrick the Snowman The cast of Jimmy Neutron build a snowman that looks just like Patrick, then Jimmy puts a magical hat on it and the snowman comes to life.
S3 December 4, 2002 Plankton's Holiday Hits A fictional commercial for Plankton's Holiday Hits album featuring parodies of songs and albums such as "Born to Run," "Deck the Halls," and "Joy to the World." At the end, an announcer provides how to "receive" the album by sending the Krabby Patty secret formula to the Chum Bucket.
S4 December 4, 2002 12 Days of Nickmas Nicktoons sing the 12 days of Christmas with their own version. Including 12 bubbles blowing, 6 jamming jellys, and a starfish on a pine tree sung by SpongeBob, Squidward, and Patrick respectively.
S5 December 4, 2002 How The You-Know-Who Stole You-Know-What! A parody of How the Grinch Stole Christmas, which featured Plankton as Cindy Lou.
S6 December 4, 2002 Nickelodeon Holiday Party Nicktoons celebrate Christmas at Santa's workshop at the north pole, as a parody of Roudolph the Red Nosed Reindeer. There was a snapshot of SpongeBob ripping his pants.
S7 December 4, 2002 A Chuckie Finster Christmas A parody of A Charlie Brown Christmas features an ad for Krabby Mint Patty advertised by Mr. Krabs.
54 January 24 2003 The Great Snail Race SpongeBob enters Gary into a race against Squidward's purebred snail.
Mid-Life Crustacean Mr. Krabs spends some overnight time with Spongebob and Patrick to neglect his mid-life crisis.
55 March 21 2003 The Sponge Who Could Fly SpongeBob tries to gain the ability to happily soar in the sky so he can befriend a jellyfish.
56 October 4 2003 Born Again Krabs Mr. Krabs is granted a second chance to be a generous boss by the Flying Dutchman.
I Had an Accident SpongeBob decides to only sit inside after he got terribly injured in a sand boarding accident.
57 January 19 2004 Plankton's Army SpongeBob tries to stay one step ahead of the villainous Plankton on the 25th anniversary of the day that he tried to steal the Krabby Patty Formula.
Missing Identity SpongeBob tries to find his missing name tag before a uniform inspection.
58 March 5 2004 SpongeBob B.C. The adventures of SpongeBob, Patrick, and Squidward's prehistoric ancestors, residents SpongeGar, Patar, and Squag.
59 April 3 2004 Krabby Land Mr. Krabs sets up a playground near the Krusty Krab, which immensly disappoints the children that visit it.
The Camping Episode Squidward joins SpongeBob and Patrick on a camping "trip" only ten feet from their houses.
60 October 11 2004 SpongeBob Meets the Strangler SpongeBob hires the maniac that is after him in disguise, for a bodyguard.
Pranks a Lot SpongeBob and Patrick feign ghosts with an invisible spray that they bought from SpongeBob's friend.

74 October 6 2006 Squidtastic Voyage SpongeBob and Patrick use Sandy's shrinking submarine to retrieve Squidward's reed after he accidentally swallowed it.
November 25, 2006 That's No Lady Patrick becomes convinced that he is in danger, so SpongeBob disguises him as a girl.
75 October 13, 2006 Bummer Vacation SpongeBob has to take a vacation so Mr. Krabs will not have to pay a fine. But he tries many schemes to get back in.
November 17, 2006 Wigstruck SpongeBob finds a wig, which annoys the Bikini Bottomites.
S8 November 2, 2006 Crossing the Street SpongeBob attempts to help an old lady cross the street, but little did he know, she wanted to take the bus. After SpongeBob is notified he runs after the bus, but trips and the old lady goes flying. She ends up using her umbrella to swing on a street light and flies into the bus, and says "Thank you" to SpongeBob. Two of the bus passengers include Tom and Fred. He gets run over by a boatmobile in the middle of replying "You're welcome!"
S9 November 2, 2006 Anything for Baby SpongeBob boards a bus, and sees that a baby is crying. So, he decides to cheer her up by making funny faces from outside the window. The baby cheers up, but the bus passes a sign in which SpongeBob is caught on.
S10 November 2, 2006 Flowers for Sandy SpongeBob is carrying a bunch of packages into his house when he realizes that it's Surprise Sandy Day. So, he sneaks over to Sandy's Treedome and places a bouquet of flowers in the vase on her picnic table with a note that says. "To Sandy, the smartest, prettiest Squirrel in Bikini Bottom. Signed: A Friend."
S11 November 2, 2006 Me Money Mr. Krabs discovers that his money is gone, so he shakes Squidward hoping to find his money. As it turns out he did not have the money, but Mr. Krabs found SpongeBob in the kitchen cleaning all the coins and paper money. Mr. Krabs then put his money in his safe, where SpongeBob was cleaning inside.
S12 November 2, 2006 Pie SpongeBob and Patrick make a pie and one ingredient is Patrick's shorts. Then Squidward eats it and they ask him if he's seen their pie "the one we spent all day making, just for you?" Squidward is guilty, and he accidentally burps out Patrick's shorts.
76 November 10 2006 Best Day Ever SpongeBob helps his friends with their problems, which ruins his "best day ever."
February 19, 2007 The Gift of Gum SpongeBob is scared by Patrick's wad of gum on Best Friend's Day, and tries to return it.
S13 2006 Gary's Gormet A clay-animated Gary eats a giant cookie.
S14 2006 Pickled Plankton A figure of Plankton is made out of vegetables.
S15 2006 Momary Gary and Momo, from Avatar: The Last Airbender, get morphed together.
S16 2006 Substitute Creature Mrs. Puff and Ms. Fowl, from The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius, get morphed together.
77 January 15, 2007 Hocus Pocus SpongeBob is convinced that he turned Squidward into an ice cream cone.
The Thing After being covered in cement, the Bikini Bottomites are convinced that Squidward is an unknown species and SpongeBob tries to keep him as a pet.
78 February 19, 2007 Driven to Tears Patrick suspects that Mrs. Puff manipulates SpongeBob's driving tests. So he decides to go to take the boating exam.
Rule of Dumb Patrick becomes king of Bikini Bottom due to his ancestors being adlings. However, during his reign, he becomes a mean tyrant.
79 February 19, 2007 The Pink Purloiner SpongeBob suspects Patrick stole his new jellyfish net.
TBA, 2007 Squid Wood SpongeBob makes a Squidward replica after he refuses to play with SpongeBob. However, the replica succeeds in all of Squidward's dreams, leading Squidward to try to get rid of him.
80 March 31, 2007 Born to Be Wild A very bad motorcycle gang is coming to Bikini Bottom and SpongeBob tries to warn everyone, but they will not listen.
Best Frenemies A new soft drink causes Plankton and Mr. Krabs to both worry about their restaurants, and they both work together to find the secret of the soft drink.
