Tennis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Tennis is a sport played with a ball and a racket. The ball is made of rubber. The goal of tennis is to hit the ball over a net. The game is played with two or four people.

The game started in England during the 1800s. Tennis is now a sport that is played at the Olympic Games. They also have the U.S. Open, Australia Open, French Open and Wimbledon.
