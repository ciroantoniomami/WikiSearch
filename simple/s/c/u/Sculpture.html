Sculpture

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Sculpture is a type of art. It must be three-dimensional (meaning it must have height, width, and depth like a cube). There are many kinds of sculptures from different parts of the word, like China and India, or from different time periods, like the renaissance and modern times. The below types of art are also thought of as sculptures:

  • Holograms
  • Pottery
  • Glass blowing
  • Costume
  • Mask
  • Doll
