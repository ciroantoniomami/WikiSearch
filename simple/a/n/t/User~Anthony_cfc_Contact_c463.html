 Contact Guidelines and Information
I serve on Wikipedia english as a Cabal and Committee Mediator, a AMA Advocate, a WelCom Welcoming Agent, a Adopt-a-User Participant, a GA judge and a anti-vandal user.

Although I would be pleased to answer your questions on the above, queries for a lot of it (especially the dispute stuff) will more than likely belong an a Wikipedia talk space page - see the icons at the side of my talk page header or, if in doubt, ask anyway. The worst I can do is point you in the right direction.

I maintain a personal neutrality policy, located here; this means I won't be commenting on contravertial topics, wheter at my talk page or otherwise. Once you've read this, feel free to leave me a message.
