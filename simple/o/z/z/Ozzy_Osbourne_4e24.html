Ozzy Osbourne

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ozzy Osbourne (born December 3, 1948) is a British heavy metal singer. He was born in Birmingham, England on December 3, 1948. He was the lead singer for Black Sabbath between 1968 and 1978. He bit the head off of a bat on stage once. His wife Sharon and he have three kids, named Aimee, Kelly and Jack. He also has three kids from his first marriage, named Elliot, Jessica, and Louis.
