Star Wars: Clone Wars is an American animated television series set in the Star Wars universe. It tell the story of what happened during the time between Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones and Star Wars Episode III: Revenge of the Sith. The series was shown on Cartoon Network between 2003 and 2005.
