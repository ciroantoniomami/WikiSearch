Wikipedia:CommonsTicker

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

CommonsTicker is the way we learn about important changes to Common's media files. Files on Commons that have license issues should either be corrected or linked to some alternate. A secondary intent of CommonsTicker is to increase the Sister-project-to-Commons communication.
