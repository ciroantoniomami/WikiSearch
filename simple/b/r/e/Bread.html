Breads

Bread is a type of food. It is made from flour and water, and usually salt and yeast. Bread is an important part of life in many countries, because everyone eats it.

In many cultures, bread is so important that it is part of their religious rituals.

Bread is also used in sandwiches as the outside and in pizza as the bottom.


There are many different kinds of bread:

  • biscuits
  • scones
  • baguettes
  • bagels
  • tortillas
  • pitas
  • lavash
  • pretzels
  • cakes
  • and many more
  • pizza dough
