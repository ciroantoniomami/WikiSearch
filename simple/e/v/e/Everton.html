Everton

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Everton Football Club (EFC) is an English football club. They are located in Liverpool, England. Everton plays at Goodison Parkin the FA Premier League. They are one of the best English clubs when it comes to major wins. The club has won the league championship nine times, the FA Cup five times and the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup once. Their last major win was the 1995 FA Cup.
