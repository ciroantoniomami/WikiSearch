Nintendo 64 was the third video game console released by Nintendo. It was released in 1996 and 1997, and was meant to compete against the Sony PlayStation and the Sega Saturn. The Nintendo 64 was Nintendo's first console with 3D graphics.

[edit] Notable games

  • The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
  • Goldeneye 007
  • Super Mario 64
