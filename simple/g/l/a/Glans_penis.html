Glans penis

From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The glans penis is the sensitive tip of the penis. It is also commonly referred to as the head of the penis. It is wholly or partially covered by the foreskin, except when the foreskin is retracted. This happens during sexual intercourse or masturbation while the penis is erect, or when the foreskin has been removed by circumcision.
